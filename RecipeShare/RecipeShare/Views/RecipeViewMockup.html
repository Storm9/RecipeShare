Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

An old standby

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  1. Spread the peanut butter on one of the slices of bread
  2. Spread the jelly on the remaining slice of bread
  3. Press the two slices of bread together so that the peanut butter and jelly are touching
